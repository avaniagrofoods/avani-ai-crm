# AVANI LOAN SERVICES - CRM Operations Guide

This document outlines the step-by-step procedures to operate every tool available in your CRM Sidebar.

## Contacts
- **Purpose**: Manage all your leads and customer data.
- **Procedure**: 
  1. Click the **Contacts** tab in the sidebar.
  2. To upload contacts in bulk, click **Import CSV**. (Ensure your CSV matches the format downloaded via **Template CSV**).
  3. To send a mass WhatsApp message to everyone, click **Send Bulk Message** and type your broadcast.
  4. To add a single contact, click **Add Contact**.

## Whatsapp Templates
- **Purpose**: Create and submit pre-approved Meta message templates (required to start conversations after 24 hours).
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open the **Whatsapp Templates** tab.
  2. Draft your template (e.g., "Hello {{1}}, your loan is approved!").
  3. Submit to Meta for approval. Once approved, use these templates to blast leads.

## Whatsapp Forms
- **Purpose**: Build native WhatsApp forms to collect data (e.g., Loan Requirements, KYC).
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **Whatsapp Forms**.
  2. Create a new form and define the required fields (Name, Loan Amount, Income).
  3. Send the form trigger to a contact in the Chatbot or Inbox.

## Tags
- **Purpose**: Segment your audience for targeted marketing.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Navigate to **Tags**.
  2. Create tags like `Hot Lead`, `Home Loan`, `Rejected`.
  3. Apply these tags to contacts in the Contacts page to easily filter them later.

## Columns
- **Purpose**: Customize the data you collect for each contact.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **Columns**.
  2. Add custom fields like `CIBIL Score`, `Monthly Income`, or `Property Value`.
  3. These fields will appear on the Contact detail view.

## Opts Management
- **Purpose**: Manage user consent to comply with WhatsApp anti-spam policies.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **Opts Management**.
  2. View users who have opted in or opted out of marketing messages.
  3. Never send bulk messages to users on the opt-out list.

## Webhook Events
- **Purpose**: Monitor incoming data from WhatsApp in real-time.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open **Webhook Events**.
  2. Watch this feed for live message delivery receipts, read receipts, and inbound texts.

## Gallery
- **Purpose**: Store media (PDFs, Images) to send to customers.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open the **Gallery**.
  2. Upload loan brochures, EMI charts, or promotional images.
  3. Attach these to your bulk messages or chatbot responses.

## FAQ Bot
- **Purpose**: Automate answers to common questions.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **FAQ Bot**.
  2. Add exact question-answer pairs (e.g., Q: "What is the ROI?", A: "Our ROI starts at 8.5%.").
  3. The bot will instantly reply when these keywords are detected.

## Chatbot
- **Purpose**: The Live 2-Way Inbox & Automated Menu Builder.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Click **Chatbot** (which routes to your Inbox).
  2. See a live view of all inbound messages from customers.
  3. Click a conversation to manually type and send a reply.

## Ai assistant
- **Purpose**: Configure Gemini AI to act as an intelligent loan advisor.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **Ai assistant**.
  2. Set the "System Prompt" to define the AI's behavior (e.g., "You are an expert loan advisor at Avani Loan Services...").

## Flows
- **Purpose**: Build complex, multi-screen UI flows inside WhatsApp.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open **Flows**.
  2. Design interactive screens for booking appointments or calculating EMIs right inside the WhatsApp chat.

## Projects & Tasks
- **Purpose**: Internal team management.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Navigate to **Projects** or **Tasks**.
  2. Assign leads or specific loan applications to your team members and track their progress.

## Settings
- **Purpose**: Configure core CRM integrations.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open **Settings**.
  2. Manage your Meta API Tokens, WhatsApp Phone Number ID, and Gemini API keys here.

## Knowledge Base
- **Purpose**: Give the AI assistant context about your specific products.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Go to **Knowledge Base**.
  2. Upload your company's policy PDFs, loan eligibility criteria, and rate charts. The AI will read these to answer customer questions accurately.

## Developers
- **Purpose**: API access for custom integrations.
- **Procedure**:
  1. Open **Developers**.
  2. Generate API keys to connect the CRM to external tools like Zapier or your website lead forms.
