## Step-by-Step Meta Approval Guide

Follow these exact steps in your **WhatsApp Business Manager** to get these approved without rejection:

### 1. Navigating to the Template Manager
1. Go to your [WhatsApp Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/).
2. Click on the **Message Templates** icon (usually looks like a message bubble) on the left sidebar.
3. Click the blue **Create Template** button in the top right.

### 2. Field-by-Field Setup
**Step 1: Category, Name, and Language**
* **Category:** Choose **Marketing** (for promotions/intros) or **Utility** (for updates/document requests). *Do not mix them up; Meta is strict about this.*
* **Name:** Name it with lowercase and underscores (e.g., `avani_welcome_message` or `loan_doc_followup`).
* **Language:** Select **English** (or English UK/US).

**Step 2: Designing the Message**
* **Header:** Select **Text**. Copy and paste the Header from the templates above. *(Alternatively, select 'Media' if you want to attach your Avani logo image, but Text gets approved faster).*
* **Body:** Copy and paste the Body text. 
  * *Important:* When you paste `{{1}}` or `{{2}}`, Meta will ask for "Sample Content" in a box on the right. 
  * For `{{1}}`, type a sample name like `Rahul`. 
  * For `{{2}}`, type a sample loan type like `Personal`. 
  * **Meta will reject the template if you don't provide sample variables!**
* **Footer:** Paste the short footer text.

**Step 3: Buttons (Crucial for Engagement)**
* Select **Quick Reply** or **Call to Action**.
* Type exactly what the button should say. If using a Website link, make sure it is exactly `https://www.avanifinserv.com/`.

### 3. Submission Best Practices
* **Grammar:** Ensure absolutely zero spelling mistakes. Meta's AI instantly rejects poor grammar.
* **No Hard Selling:** Never use words like "BUY NOW", "DISCOUNT", or excessive emojis (🔥🔥🔥). 
* **Submit:** Click the blue **Submit** button. Approval usually takes anywhere from 2 minutes to 24 hours. Once the dot turns green, it is ready to use in your CRM!
